## Data Sources

Raw and adapted payloads from the following sources are intentionally excluded from normal Git
history and remain subject to their current upstream licenses and repository terms:

- `nvidia/Open-SWE-Traces`;
- `sierra-research/tau-bench` historical trajectories;
- `princeton-nlp/SWE-bench_Lite` and content originating from its underlying repositories.

The U3/U4 and T3 controlled overlays retain an upstream trace substrate. Their synthetic labels do
not relicense the underlying prompt, tool, code, or repository content. Consult
`artifacts/dataset-manifest.json` and the dataset cards before redistribution.

The fixture under `examples/tool_agent_smoke/` and Synthetic Dataset A are generated by this project
and contain no copied upstream prompt rows.
